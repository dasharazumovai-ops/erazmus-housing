Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose an apartment?

Use the area pages to compare listings and open the apartment details for more information.

How can I contact you?

You can contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or social media using the contact details provided on the website.

Can I book before arriving in Cyprus?

Yes, many students arrange their accommodation before they arrive.

Do I need a deposit to secure a room?

A deposit equal to one month’s rent is required to secure a room.

Do all apartments include the same features?

No, features vary by apartment, so please check each listing carefully.
