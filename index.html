About Us

Erasmus Life Nicosia Housing helps Erasmus and international students find trusted accommodation in Nicosia. Since 2015, we have focused on helping students feel more confident when searching for a room or flat in a new city.

We specialize in student housing in the areas most relevant to university life, including locations near UNIC, EUC, and other key parts of Nicosia. Our aim is to make the process simpler by offering verified options and clear information to help students choose the right home.

We understand that moving abroad can feel stressful, so we want the accommodation search to feel more personal, reliable, and student-friendly. Whether you are arranging your room before arriving in Cyprus or looking for a place after arrival, we are here to help you find a home in Nicosia.

Discover places, food, and experiences around Nicosia and beyond.

Historical & Places to See

Cyprus Folk Art Museum

A quiet escape from the city where you can see how people in Cyprus used to live, from handmade textiles to traditional crafts. Surprisingly interesting, even if museums aren’t usually your thing.

Ledra Street

This is where you’ll probably end up more than you expect, whether it’s for coffee, shopping or just walking around with friends.

The Green Line

Right in the middle of the city, you’ll come across the line that still divides Cyprus today. It’s a strange but fascinating place that makes you realise how unique Nicosia really is.

Archaelogical site of Palaion Demarcheion

A hidden spot in the old city where you can walk through real ruins and see how Nicosia looked centuries ago. It’s small, easy to explore, and perfect if you want something different from the usual cafés and streets.

Beaches & Nature

Athalassa Park

The largest park Nicosia to escape the city and just slow down for a bit. Perfect for walks, bike rides or a relaxed afternoon with friends around the lake.

Nissi Beach

Around an hour from Nicosia in Agia Napa, this beach is famous for its crystal-clear water and lively atmosphere.

Mackenzie Beach

Located about 40 minutes from Nicosia, this beach is perfect for a more relaxed vibe with cafés and restaurants by the sea. You can even watch planes land right above you, which makes it a really unique experience.

Eleftheria Square

A modern green, open space in the centre of Nicosia where people meet, relax and hang out. Great for a quick study break or just enjoying the atmosphere.

Restaurants & Cafés

Fanous Lebanese Restaurant

A popular spot known for generous portions and authentic Lebanese flavours. Even better, they offer discounts for Erasmus students, making it a go-to for group dinners.

JK One Resto-bar

A lively restobar with a slight Asian-inspired vibe, known for its unlimited sushi nights hosted by ELN. More than just food, it often turns into a social spot with a bar and club-like atmosphere, making it a favourite for Erasmus students.

Piatsa Gourounaki

A go-to spot for authentic Greek street food, served fresh and full of flavour. Great for a quick bite with friends, especially when you want something satisfying without spending too much.

Rami's cafe

A relaxed café with a cosy atmosphere, perfect for coffee, light food or catching up with friends.
