About Us

Erasmus Life Nicosia Housing helps Erasmus and international students find trusted accommodation in Nicosia. Since 2015, we have focused on helping students feel more confident when searching for a room or flat in a new city.

We specialize in student housing in the areas most relevant to university life, including locations near UNIC, EUC, and other key parts of Nicosia. Our aim is to make the process simpler by offering verified options and clear information to help students choose the right home.

We understand that moving abroad can feel stressful, so we want the accommodation search to feel more personal, reliable, and student-friendly. Whether you are arranging your room before arriving in Cyprus or looking for a place after arrival, we are here to help you find a home in Nicosia.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose an apartment?

Use the area pages to compare listings and open the apartment details for more information.

How can I contact you?

You can contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or social media using the contact details provided on the website.

Can I book before arriving in Cyprus?

Yes, many students arrange their accommodation before they arrive.

Do I need a deposit to secure a room?

A deposit equal to one month’s rent is required to secure a room.

Do all apartments include the same features?

No, features vary by apartment, so please check each listing carefully.
